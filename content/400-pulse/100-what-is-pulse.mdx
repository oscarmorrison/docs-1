<TopBlock>

[Prisma Pulse](https://www.prisma.io/data-platform/pulse) is a managed [change data capture (CDC)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Change_data_capture) service that captures and distributes your database events to your application. Subscribe to type-safe data changes with and extended [Prisma Client](/orm/prisma-client) to power real-time functionality like chat, data synchronization, notifications, and more.

</TopBlock>
